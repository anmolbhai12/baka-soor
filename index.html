Our story & values

Craft, community, quality — baked into every loaf.

La Maison began as a neighbourhood bakehouse with a simple mission: bake the best bread possible and share it. We handcraft everything in small batches, using organic flour, natural leavening, and seasonal produce. Our bakers train in traditional methods and are driven by flavour and texture.

  • Traditional techniques: long fermentation, careful hydration.
  • Local partnerships: seasonal produce & sustainably sourced dairy.
  • Bespoke orders: weddings, corporate, wholesale.
